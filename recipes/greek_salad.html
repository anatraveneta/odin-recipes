Greek Salad

Description

The Mediterranean serves as an example to radiate freshness from its dishes. In this case we make a Greek version where feta cheese and black olives are not lacking.

You can flirt with some other fresh and hard cheese or replace the olives with others that you prefer, but this mixture is a success at any time.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes: You'll need four of them, raher riped.
  • Cucumnber: With one it is enough, unles your are a big fan of them!
  • Green pepper: Choose one from your local provider. They're so much tastier!
  • Onion: The red ones add a colourful touch. They're maybe softer also. Challotes are also a good alternative. One piece will be enough.
  • Black olives: Here you can put the amount you want. I personnally love the pickled greek ones.
  • Feta cheese: For four people, 50 gr should be enough. Be sure to get the authentic one!
  • Oil: What could be a Mediterranean salad without it?
  • Salt: A pinch of salt brings it all together.
  • Oregano: This herb will make the difference!.

Steps

  1. Cut the cucumber, the pepper and the tomato into not very small cubes and the onion into medium strips.
  2. Mix everything well in a bowl, with a little salt and a generous splash of olive oil.
  3. Add the olives and finish with the diced feta cheese.
  4. Sprinkle a little oregano.
  5. Squeeze a little lemon if you want.
